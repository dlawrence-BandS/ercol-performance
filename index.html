Engagement & Soft Metrics

The add-to-basket rate has climbed from 0.41% during the onsite-only phase to above the pre-optimisation baseline. Measured year-on-year, the ATC rate has risen from 0.50% to 0.67% — a +34% like-for-like improvement. Engagement rate has improved consistently across all three phases, confirming that content changes have made pages more relevant.

Sales & Revenue Performance

The full-year daily average is distorted by Black Friday and January peaks. The YoY lens gives a cleaner read: April 2026 was down as new pages had barely a week of indexing, but May 2026 revenue was up +22.6% YoY. The post-expansion period (70 purchases over 57 days) vs onsite-only (7 purchases over 24 days) demonstrates that product catalogue depth is the primary conversion driver.

Brand Position

Ercol is the #1 revenue brand on site — contributing over 27% of all brand-attributed revenue. It leads Simba by 68% and Pure Furniture by 88%. Simba's April 2026 spike was promotional and temporary. Ercol's performance is structurally consistent and growing.
